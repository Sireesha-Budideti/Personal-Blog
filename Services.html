Performance Testing

In today’s fast-paced digital landscape, performance is crucial for user satisfaction and retention.

  • Load Testing: Simulating multiple users to assess how applications perform under heavy traffic.
  • Stress Testing: Pushing applications beyond normal operational capacity to determine their breaking points.
  • Endurance Testing: Evaluating applications’ performance over extended periods to ensure stability.
  • Scalability Testing: Assessing applications' ability to scale up or down based on demand.
  • Performance Monitoring: Implementing tools to continuously monitor application performance in real-time.
